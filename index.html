Golden Retriever Dogs

Dogs belonging to the golden retriever breed are smart family dogs. They have only one request from you: Love. They are hungry to be loved. No matter how much you love them, they get grumpy when you take your hand off them. You will always care and love. I think being loved is more important than food and water for them.

Difficulties of Golden Retriever Dogs

  • They shed more than you can imagine.
  • They are so energetic dogs and they can fall and get injured.
